# Game Description

## 1. Title

**Chicken Run Adventure**

## 2. Language

English

## 3. Developer

@try_soon_late

## 4. Short Description

Run, jump, and duck your way through an endless adventure! Control a brave chicken, avoid obstacles, collect eggs and seeds, and survive as long as you can in this fast-paced runner game with day and night cycles.

## 5. Full Description

Embark on an endless running adventure with Chicken Run Adventure! Take control of a courageous chicken as you dash through a dynamically changing landscape filled with challenges and rewards.

**Gameplay:**
- Master simple yet addictive controls: jump over ground obstacles and duck under aerial threats
- Navigate through stones on the ground and avoid enemy birds flying overhead
- Collect eggs to boost your score and seeds for extra bonuses
- Survive as long as possible - the game speed increases over time, making each run more challenging

**Features:**
- **Dynamic Day/Night Cycle**: Experience beautiful transitions from day to night as you play
- **Progressive Difficulty**: Game speed gradually increases, testing your reflexes and skills
- **Life System**: You have 3 lives - use them wisely to survive longer
- **Score Tracking**: Beat your best score and compete with yourself to go further each time
- **Smooth Controls**: Responsive touch controls with intuitive jump and duck mechanics
- **Adaptive Screen**: Works seamlessly in both portrait and landscape orientations

**Challenges:**
Watch out for ground obstacles like stones that require you to jump, and enemy birds that fly at jump height - duck when needed! The faster you go, the more obstacles appear, so stay focused and react quickly.

Can you survive the longest and achieve the highest score? Jump into Chicken Run Adventure and put your running skills to the test!
